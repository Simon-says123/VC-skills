# analysing-competition

## What it does
Maps the competitive landscape for a specific startup under active due diligence, producing a structured IC-ready report that includes a competitor map, differentiation assessment, moat scoring across six dimensions, competitive risk scenarios, and a clear investment verdict. This is a stand-alone skill scoped to competitive analysis only — it is not part of the screening or startup evaluation workflows and can be run independently at any point during a deal process.

## Reference files
- `references/moat-framework.md` — canonical moat taxonomy, scoring guide, and red flags
- `references/competitive-map-template.md` — competitor table format and column definitions
- `references/ic-competitive-checklist.md` — IC pre-flight self-review questions
- `[company context file]` — your firm's investment lens and CVC participation risk flags
- `[company divisions file]` — internal divisions for strategic backer conflict checks

## Install
Copy this folder into your skills directory, then toggle the skill on in **Customize > Skills**.

## What to change
- `[company context file]` — replace with your own firm context and investment criteria
- `[company divisions file]` — replace with your own firm's divisions or strategic arms for conflict-of-interest checks
- "Prepared by: [FUND NAME]" in SKILL.md — update to reflect your fund or team name

## Usage
**Trigger:** "competitive analysis for [company]"
**Output:** PDF competitive analysis report
